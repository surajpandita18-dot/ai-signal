# Persona: The Indie Builder

**Name:** Karan / Pieter / Ana (archetype)

**Role:** Solo founder or 2-person bootstrapped startup. Technical. Building something in AI, often nights/weekends alongside a day job or with runway < 12 months.

**Stage:** Pre-revenue or < $3k MRR. Every dollar spent is felt.

---

## Day in the life

- No standup, no team Slack — works alone
- Checks Twitter/X, HN, and one or two newsletters in the morning
- Deep work blocks of 4–6 hours — minimal context switching
- Reads more AI news than a funded founder (more time, more curiosity)
- **Total time for AI news: 20–30 minutes daily — but highly selective on tools**

---

## Current information diet

- Twitter/X: their main signal source — curated follows, no algorithm
- HN front page: reliable filter for what technically matters
- Free newsletters: TLDR, Latent Space, maybe one arXiv digest
- Reddit: r/MachineLearning, r/LocalLLaMA for open-source signals
- **Problem:** Too much noise, no one curating for "what does this mean for a 1-person shop trying to ship fast with limited compute budget?"

---

## Jobs-to-be-done

**Primary:** "Tell me if I should switch what I'm building on top of — before I waste another sprint on the wrong foundation."

**Secondary:** "Help me sound credible when talking to investors or users about where the market is going."

**Tertiary:** "Find the open-source drop that lets me match a feature I was about to build myself."

---

## Relationship to AI Signal

- Will use free tier for weeks before considering upgrade
- Upgrade trigger: a TAKEAWAY that saves them a sprint of wasted work
- Price sensitivity: $49/month feels high — $19/month is "obvious yes"
- Most likely to share on Twitter/X if something is genuinely useful (high virality value)
- Bookmarks heavily — "save for later" is their information management strategy

---

## Upgrade triggers

- Reads a TAKEAWAY that directly prevents a wrong technical decision
- Uses the save feature as a personal research log (5+ saves)
- Finds AI Signal before their peers know about it (early adopter identity)

---

## Objections to upgrade

- "I can piece this together for free across my existing sources"
- "$49/month is 10% of my MRR right now"
- "I'm not sure I'll check it consistently enough to justify it"
- "Is this actually specific to what I'm building, or generic AI news with a better UI?"

---

## Verbatim language

- "Is this open source or paid?"
- "What does this mean for someone building solo?"
- "Will this be obsolete in 3 months?"
- "Can I self-host this or do I need the API?"
- "Is the free tier actually useful or is it bait?"
