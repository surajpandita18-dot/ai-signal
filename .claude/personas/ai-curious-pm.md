# Persona: The AI-Adjacent Product Manager

**Name:** Priya / Jordan / Tomas (archetype)

**Role:** Senior PM or Head of Product at a company adopting AI (not an AI-native startup)

**Context:** Their company is not an AI lab, but AI is reshaping their product. They are the person responsible for deciding *which* AI capabilities to integrate and when.

---

## Day in the life

- Morning: product metrics review, Slack catch-up
- Reads newsletters but rarely before 10am
- Skims Twitter for "what's trending in AI" — not deep research
- Has weekly AI strategy meeting with CTO/engineering lead
- **Total time for AI news: 15–20 minutes spread across the day**

---

## Current information diet

- Morning Brew / TLDR Tech: covers headlines, not technical depth
- Twitter/X: mostly commentary, not original sources
- Company Slack: engineering team shares stuff occasionally
- **Problem:** Can't evaluate technical claims. Needs the "so what" translated into product decisions.

---

## Jobs-to-be-done

**Primary:** "Tell me what to bring to the engineering meeting this week that's actually worth discussing."

**Secondary:** "Help me sound informed about AI without having to become an engineer."

**Tertiary:** "Spot the signal in my industry before my VP asks me about it."

---

## Relationship to AI Signal

- Less likely to pay individually — might expense it
- Highly likely to share with team via Slack if something is relevant
- Values the "Why it matters" section more than the TAKEAWAY (they can't always act on technical specifics)
- Activation moment: a signal that directly maps to a product decision they are currently facing

---

## Upgrade triggers

- Uses "save" feature to build a personal research library
- Shares 3+ signals with teammates in first week (virality signal)
- A signal helps them make a case in a meeting

---

## Verbatim language they use

- "Is this something we need to react to?"
- "How does this compare to what we're already using?"
- "What should I tell the engineering team?"
- "Is this a 6-month horizon thing or do we need to move now?"
