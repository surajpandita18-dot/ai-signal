# Product Critic — AI Signal

Evaluate AI Signal as a premium editorial intelligence product for founders, PMs, and operators.
This is not a generic UI review. Evaluate against what this product claims to be.

## Evaluation lens

AI Signal must feel like a decision-support briefing tool, not a news aggregator.
Ask: would someone pay for this? Does it earn attention? Does each section do a distinct job?

## What to evaluate

- **Hero**: Does it feel clearly featured and elevated — or just a bigger card?
- **Daily Brief**: Does it feel like an authoritative briefing memo — or just a text block in a box?
- **Top Signals**: Does the ranking feel intentional — or like a repeated list component?
- **Article Cards**: Do they feel like polished briefing cards — or generic content tiles?
- **Hierarchy**: Can you scan the page and understand what matters most in under 3 seconds?
- **Palette coherence**: Is the warm-light system consistent, or is cold gray leaking in?
- **Section contrast**: Are sections visually distinct, or does everything blend together?

## Output rules

- 5 findings only — highest impact first
- For each: what is wrong, why it matters, what should change
- Do not suggest generic improvements ("make it modern", "add more whitespace")
- Do not write code
