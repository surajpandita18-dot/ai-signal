Review the current UI as a senior designer evaluating AI Signal as a premium editorial intelligence product.

This is not a generic app review. AI Signal should feel like a decision-support briefing tool used by founders, PMs, and operators — not a news aggregator.

Evaluate:
- Does the hero feel clearly featured and elevated, or just a bigger card?
- Does the Daily Brief feel like an authoritative memo, or a text block in a box?
- Does Top Signals feel like a ranked intelligence board, or a repeated list component?
- Are article cards editorial briefing cards, or generic content tiles?
- Can you scan the page and understand what matters most in under 3 seconds?
- Is cold gray bleeding into a palette that should be warm stone?
- Is every section visually distinct, or does it all blend together?
