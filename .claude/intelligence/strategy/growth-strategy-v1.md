# AI Signal — Growth & Distribution Strategy v1

**Date:** May 14, 2026  
**Status:** Living doc — refine weekly based on metrics

## Audience

**Primary tiers (mix):**
- Indian PMs at Series A-C startups
- Indian AI builders/developers
- Indian founders (early-stage)

**Common need:** AI signal, not noise. Save time. Ship better.

**Common pain:** Information overload. 50+ AI updates daily. No curation.

## Platform Priority

**LinkedIn (Primary)**
- 85% of target audience active daily
- B2B audience native
- Long-form content welcome
- Best for AI Signal positioning
- Higher subscriber conversion potential

**Instagram (Secondary, Unique Growth Channel)**
- 50% target audience active
- Younger builders + founders
- Visual brand building
- Discovery engine
- Reach beyond LinkedIn network

**Cross-funnel: Both → Website → Subscribe**  
Newsletter = central asset.

## Content Mix (80/20)

### LinkedIn — Daily (7 posts/week)
- 5x article carousel (one per day's signal)
- 1x personal founder note (build-in-public)
- 1x lessons/insights (different format)

### Instagram — Daily (7 posts/week)
- 5x article carousel (adapted format for IG)
- 1x AI tech meme/relatable humor
- 1x "behind the scenes" story or quick thought
- Daily Stories: sneak peek, polls, quick takes

### Why this mix
- 100% article = boring, low engagement
- 100% lifestyle = no audience-product fit
- 80/20 = value depth + brand personality

## Independent Growth Tactics

### LinkedIn
- Daily commenting (10 relevant posts/day, 5 min each)
- Engage with target audience accounts
- Tag relevant people thoughtfully
- Share carousels with thoughtful captions
- Build personal brand alongside AI Signal
- Connect with new subscribers
- Weekly: 1 longer-form personal post

### Instagram
- Reels: 30-sec AI insights, 3x/week
- Hashtag strategy: 
  #AI #ProductManagement #IndiaTech 
  #AIIndia #StartupIndia #BuildInPublic
- Engage with similar accounts daily
- Story polls + Q&A weekly
- Collaborate with creators
