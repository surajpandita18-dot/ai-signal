# AI Signal — Product Context

## Product Purpose
Daily AI newsletter. One signal per day — the single most important thing in AI, published at 06:14 IST, gone in 24 hours. Not a roundup. Not a feed. One pick, editorially curated by Suraj Pandita.

## Register
brand — the homepage IS the product. Design drives trust and urgency.

## Users
1. **Product managers** at Indian and global tech companies — need the signal to prep for standups, roadmap reviews, board decks
2. **Founders** — need to know what shifts their competitive landscape overnight
3. **Engineers/builders** — need to understand model changes, pricing shifts, and new primitives that affect their stack
4. **Curious professionals** — people who want to stay current without doom-scrolling

Average reader: senior IC or early founder, 28–38, Indian tech ecosystem, reads in the morning before meetings.

## Brand
- Voice: authoritative, brief, no hedging. Editorial tone of a Bloomberg terminal crossed with a Bengaluru startup WhatsApp group.
- Typography: Instrument Serif for display (premium, editorial), JetBrains Mono for metadata (technical credibility), Caveat for handwritten moments (human signal in a world of AI slop)
- Color: Warm cream base (#FAFAF7), signal blue (#2B5BFF) for the primary accent, warm orange (#FF6B35) for urgency/expiry/live indicators
- Feel: The Wall Street Journal if it were built by a 28-year-old in Koramangala. Premium but approachable. Dense but not cluttered.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS landing pages (Stripe/Vercel clones)
- Dark mode everything
- Rounded corners on everything
- "AI-generated" aesthetic (gradient blobs without purpose, neon colors, futuristic fonts)
- Newsletter templates that look like Substack

## Strategic principles
1. **The 24h window is the product.** Every design decision must reinforce scarcity and urgency without feeling manipulative.
2. **Signal numbers are currency.** "Signal #1" creates community memory. Numbers > dates in all UI.
3. **Every word costs.** No padding copy. If the signal is good, it speaks for itself.
4. **Trust through restraint.** The editorial judgement ("nothing cleared the bar today") IS the value proposition.

## Key pages
- `/` — homepage, always today's signal (or expired/empty/no-signal states)
- `/signal/[number]` — individual signal page
- `/archive` — subscriber-only archive (Phase 5)
- `/about` — editorial philosophy
- `/admin` — internal compose tool (Phase 6)
